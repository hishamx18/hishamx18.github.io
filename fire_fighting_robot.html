Fire Fighting Robot

Arduino-based microcontroller fire fighting robot with IR flame sensors.

Details

This project utilizes an Arduino microcontroller along with IR flame sensors to detect and extinguish fire. The robot autonomously navigates towards the fire source and sprays water to put it out.


Skills learnt

Arduino IDE, Motor Drivers, Flame Sensors
