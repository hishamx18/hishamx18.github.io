Leo Rover

Details

A robot that can move autonomously by using Simultaneous Localisation and Mapping (SLAM), avoiding obstacles, identify colored blocks, pick and place them into the matching colored storage bins. My role was to design the required parts to 3D print for the rover and object picking and placing using the Interbotix PX 100 manipulator.


Skills learnt

ROS 2, Python, Solidworks, RVIZ, Gazebo, Nav2
